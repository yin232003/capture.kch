Foodie Tips

Best Time to Eat

Visit hawker centers and food courts between 6-8pm for the freshest selections and liveliest atmosphere.

Budget-Friendly

Most local dishes cost RM8-20. Bring cash as many street vendors don't accept cards.

Spice Levels

Don't hesitate to ask for "kurang pedas" (less spicy) if you're sensitive to heat. Most vendors are happy to adjust.

Halal Options

Many establishments offer halal versions of popular dishes. Look for halal certification or ask the vendor.
