Traditional Arts & Crafts

Pua Kumbu Weaving

Intricate Iban weaving with symbolic patterns representing nature and mythology.

Materials: Cotton, natural dyes Where to find: Sarawak Cultural Village, local markets

Sape Music

Traditional lute-like instrument played by Orang Ulu communities, now gaining international recognition.

Materials: Belian wood, snake skin Where to hear: Cultural performances, local festivals

Woodcarving

Elaborate carvings on longhouse posts, masks, and utensils with spiritual significance.

Materials: Belian, ramin wood Where to see: State Museum, artisan workshops

Beadwork

Colorful bead jewelry and accessories created by Kenyah and Kayan women.

Materials: Glass beads, seeds, shells Where to buy: Handicraft markets, cultural centers
