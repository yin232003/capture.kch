Bako National Park

Sarawak's oldest protected area (27 sq km). Mangroves, limestone cliffs, wildlife including bearded pigs and silvered leaf monkeys.

Travel Hack

Share a boat at Bako Terminal to reduce cost (RM40 vs RM200 solo).

Semenggoh Wildlife Rehabilitation Centre

Home to rescued orangutans. Visit during feedings (10am/3pm).

Important Note

Maintain 5m distance from orangutans.

Kubah National Park

The park, which spans 2,230 hectares, is home to three notable sandstone peaks that can be seen from the city on clear days: Gunung Serapi, Gunung Selang, and Gunung Sendok.

Travel Hack

A number of travel operators in Kuching run guided tours to the park.
Entrance Fee: RM20

Wind Caves

Part of the Bau Formation, a narrow belt of limestone covering about 150 square kilometres of Southwest Sarawak.

Important Note

You will need a flashlight.

Jong Crocodile Farm

The finest & largest captive breeding crocodile farm in Malaysia. Visitors can have the enchanting experience of walking freely among the Jungle of Borneo.

Travel Hack

Adult: RM 25 per pax.
Children 3 to 11 years old: RM 12 per pax.
Children below 3: Free
